About Me

My is Daniel - Software Development student at BYU-Idaho, where I’m learning how to build modern, responsive, and scalable web applications. I have a growing passion for technology, problem-solving, and creating tools that make people’s lives easier. I’m gaining real-world experience by applying what I learn in class to personal projects, collaborative assignments, and hands-on coding challenges. I enjoy working with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and I’m also learning back-end technologies and version control tools like Git and GitHub. My goal is to become a full-stack developer, contribute to open-source projects, and one day build software that solves real problems in Africa and beyond.



Nigeria

Official flag of Nigeria

Nigeria is a vibrant and diverse country located in West Africa, known for its rich cultural heritage and over 500 spoken languages. It is the most populous country in Africa and has a fast-growing economy fueled by industries like oil, agriculture, and technology. Nigeria is home to major cities such as Lagos, Abuja (the capital), and Kano, each offering unique contributions to the nation's identity. With its mix of tradition and innovation, Nigeria plays a key role in shaping Africa’s cultural and economic future.
